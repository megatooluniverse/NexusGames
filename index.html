How To Play P2P

  • This app uses WebRTC. No central game server exists.
  • Player 1 (Host) creates a room and sends the link/code.
  • Player 2 (Guest) opens the link to establish a direct connection.
  • Once connected, the connection is Peer-to-Peer. It's lightning fast!
  • Do not close the browser tab, or the connection will drop.
